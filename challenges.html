EcoCharge

Challenges

Conserve energy

  • • Turn off lights when you leave a room
  • • Unplug electronics when not in use

Save Water

  • • Take shorter showers
  • • Fix leaks
  • • Track how much water is used when washing dishes or doing laundry

Reduce Waste

  • • Avoid single-use plastics like straws and water bottles, use reusable bags and containers
  • • Separate waste for recycling

Food Waste

  • • Plan meals in advance
  • • Store food properly
  • • Compost food scraps and consider eating more plant-based meals

Transportation

  • • Walk
  • • Bike
  • • Carpool
  • • Use public transportation when possible instead of driving alone
