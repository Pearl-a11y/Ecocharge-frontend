## About EcoCharge

EcoCharge is an interactive and educational web app that teaches users about renewable energy and sustainable living through:
- Interactive video lessons
- Gamified quizzes
- Real-world eco-challenges
- Progress tracking with badges

## Problem Statement

Many young people care about the environment but find learning about renewable energy boring or abstract. EcoCharge makes sustainability education engaging through gamification and practical challenges.

## Target Users

- Environmentally conscious individuals aged 13-25
- Students learning about sustainability
- People wanting to reduce their carbon footprint

## Features

- **Interactive Lessons:** Video content about renewable energy
- **Quizzes:** Test knowledge on sustainability topics
- **Challenges:** Practical eco-missions to complete in real life
- **Progress Tracking:** Earn points and unlock badges
- **Community Impact:** View global rankings and achievements

## Technologies Used

- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript (Vanilla)
- Responsive Design
